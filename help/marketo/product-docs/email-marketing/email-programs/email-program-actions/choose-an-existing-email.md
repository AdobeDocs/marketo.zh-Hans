# 选择现有电子邮件 {#choose-an-existing-email}

>[!PREREQUISITES]
>
>* [创建电子邮件程序](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/creating-an-email-program/create-an-email-program.md)
>* [使用智能列表定义受众](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/managing-people-in-email-programs/define-an-audience-with-a-smart-list.md) 或 [通过导入列表定义受众](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/managing-people-in-email-programs/define-an-audience-by-importing-a-list.md)


一旦 [创建了电子邮件程序](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/creating-an-email-program/create-an-email-program.md) 定义受众后，您将需要决定发送的电子邮件。 您可以 [为电子邮件程序创建电子邮件](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-actions/create-an-email-for-an-email-program.md) 从头开始，或者选择一个已经存在的。 下面是如何选择已经存在的。

1. 转到 **营销活动**.

   ![](assets/login-marketing-activities.png)

1. 查找并选择您的电子邮件程序。

   ![](assets/selectemailprogram.jpg)

1. 在 **电子邮件** 拼贴，选择要发送的拼贴。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-12-11-3a28-3a10.png)

   >[!NOTE]
   >
   >只能选择本地电子邮件。 需要将电子邮件从一个项目移动到另一个项目？ [了解此处](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-actions/move-an-email.md).

   真贴心！

   ![](assets/image2014-9-12-11-3a28-3a51.png)

既然我们决定了要发送哪封电子邮件，那么我们可以设置A/B测试，或者跳过该测试并计划电子邮件程序。

>[!MORELIKETHIS]
>
>* [添加A/B测试](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-actions/email-test-a-b-test/add-an-a-b-test.md)
>* [计划电子邮件程序](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-actions/schedule-your-email-program.md)
