# 跨工作区报告电子邮件、营销活动效果 {#report-email-campaign-performance-across-workspaces}

启用全局报告以在[电子邮件性能](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-data/email-performance-report.md)和[电子邮件链接性能](/help/marketo/product-docs/email-marketing/email-programs/email-program-data/email-link-performance-report.md)报告中包含来自所有Marketo [工作区](/help/marketo/product-docs/administration/workspaces-and-person-partitions/create-a-new-workspace.md)的数据。

1. 转到&#x200B;**Analytics**（或&#x200B;**营销活动**）区域。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-16-3a4-3a46.png)

1. 选择您的报表。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-16-3a4-3a51.png)

1. 单击&#x200B;**设置**&#x200B;选项卡，然后双击&#x200B;**全局报告**。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-16-3a4-3a58.png)

1. 选择&#x200B;**已启用**。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-16-3a5-3a4.png)

1. 仅此而已！ 单击“**报表**”选项卡可查看来自所有工作区的数据。

   ![](assets/image2014-9-16-16-3a5-3a8.png)

   >[!MORELIKETHIS]
   >
   >[在电子邮件报告中筛选Assets](/help/marketo/product-docs/reporting/basic-reporting/report-activity/filter-assets-in-an-email-report.md)
