# Publish自定义活动 {#publish-a-custom-activity}

您的自定义活动完全符合您的需要。 现在，该发布它了！

1. 转到&#x200B;**[!UICONTROL 管理员]**&#x200B;区域。

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-1.png)

1. 单击&#x200B;**[!UICONTROL Marketo自定义活动]**。

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-2.png)

1. 选择要发布的自定义活动。

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-3.png)

1. 单击&#x200B;**[!UICONTROL 自定义活动操作]**&#x200B;下拉列表，然后选择&#x200B;**[!UICONTROL Publish活动]**。

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-4.png)

   您将看到自定义活动的[!UICONTROL 状态]从[!UICONTROL 草稿]开始……

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-5.png)

   ...到[!UICONTROL 已发布]。

   ![](assets/publish-a-custom-activity-6.png)

   做得好！
